Jaguar

Jaguars have the strongest bite of any big cat, able to crush skulls and shells with ease.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera onca
  • Average Length: 5 to 6 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 12 to 15 years
  • Habitat: Tropical rainforests

Jaguars are powerful big cats with strong bites and distinctive spotted coats, mainly found in Central and South American rainforests.
